Mission Statement

This website is dedicated to addressing the global challenge of food loss and waste through informed analysis and data-driven insights. By providing detailed information on food loss statistics across various stages of the food supply chain, we aim to empower stakeholders to make better decisions that can lead to more sustainable food consumption and production practices.

How to Use This Site

Explore interactive data visualizations to understand trends in food loss and waste. Use filters to view data by country, year, and type of food product. Gain insights into problem areas and identify opportunities for improvement in the food supply chain.

Our Target Personas

  • Policy Makers: Who can drive change at the legislative level by understanding the data trends and enacting relevant policies.
  • Food Industry Professionals: Who require data to optimize processes and reduce waste in production and distribution phases.
  • Environmental Advocates: Who need evidence to support campaigns for more sustainable food systems.
  • Academic Researchers: Who are studying food sustainability and looking for data to support their research.
